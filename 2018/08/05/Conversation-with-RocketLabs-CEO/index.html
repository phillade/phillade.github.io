Conversation with Rocket Lab CEO Peter Beck
Created 2018-08-05| Updated 2019-11-13|entrepreneurship

Lesson 1 Bootstraps are your friend

Before Rocket Lab’s billion dollar evaluation, Peter bootstrapped together different rocket engineer designs with his friends. It took him many years before he finally arrived at Silicon Valley raising venture funding for his vision. A future with frequent small scale satellite launches.

Lesson 2 Hire people who believe in the mission

Having a very high GPA could be an indicator of success. But what matters more is hiring people who truly believe in the mission. It’s much easier to say than being done, but to this day, Peter still hand signs every single new hire.
